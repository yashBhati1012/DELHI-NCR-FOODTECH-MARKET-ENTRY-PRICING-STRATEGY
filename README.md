# 🛵 Zomato-Delhi-NCR-FoodTech-Dashboard

## 🚀 1. Project Title 
### 🍕 Delhi-NCR Food-Tech Market Entry & Pricing Strategy Dashboard
A dynamic, interactive business intelligence tool built to explore regional food technology footprints, pricing models, and marketplace dynamics across Delhi-NCR—focusing on localized competitive advantages, micro-market positioning, and demand-supply gaps.

---

## 📌 2. Short Description
The **Delhi-NCR Food-Tech Dashboard** is an enterprise-grade analytics platform designed to enable restaurant executives, product managers, and hospitality investors to evaluate new market entry variables. By parsing pricing benchmarks, user ratings, and geographic constraints, the system clarifies hyper-local food trends to mitigate risk profiles associated with restaurant distribution planning.

---

## 🛠️ 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard and data ingestion layer utilize the following technologies:
* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Core development platform for visualization orchestration and presentation architecture.
* 🧼 **Power Query** – Advanced ETL processing layer engineered for data sanitation and formatting.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Deployed for contextual measures, metric aggregations, and variable calculations.
* 🧱 **Data Modeling** – Structured relational database schemas optimized for cross-table performance.
* 📁 **File Formats** – `.pbix` for active workspace logic and `.png`/`.jpg` for presentation previews.

---

## 🗃️ 4. Data Source
* **Origin Schema:** Multi-source transactional grids capturing operational variables from restaurant listings across primary hubs.
* **Volume Profiles:** Comprehensive row registers mapping structural location metrics (`Locality`, `Latitude`, `Longitude`), financial metrics (`Pricing_for_2`), and public feedback parameters (`Dining_Rating`).

## 🎯 5. Features 

### 💼 Business Problem
Entering a saturated geographic ecosystem like Delhi-NCR presents major operational risk. New food businesses often fail due to unstructured location selection, mismatched pricing structures, and failing to spot heavy competition.

### 🏁 Goal of the Dashboard
To build a functional, single-page diagnostic interface that visualizes regional pricing trends, evaluates saturation limits per cuisine, and targets profitable geographic placement opportunities.

### 🔍 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
* **Dynamic Slicer Panel:** Empowers real-time sorting across distinct geographic regions (e.g., Saket, Noida, Gurgaon, Jama Masjid) to filter metrics in one click.
* **Core KPI Cards:** Direct readout tiles tracking `Average Price for Two` and `Average Dining Rating` for immediate contextual baselines.
* **Dynamic Selection Indicator:** Interactive indicator badges that update automatically to display current categorical segments and prevent dashboard user disorientation.
* **Proportional Market Ring (Donut Visual):** Highlights exact saturation metrics by indexing active category distributions with unified text detail labels.
* **Geographic Pinpoint Tracker:** Mapping visual plotting specific geographic density tags to locate market footprints instantly.

### 💡 Business Impact & Insights
* **Pricing Threshold Optimization:** Instantly distinguishes variance in pricing setups across premium commercial centers versus cultural micro-zones.
* **Saturation-Gap Discovery:** Identifies underrepresented cuisine types inside highly targeted local market clusters.
* **Targeted Capital Deployment:** Enables corporate planners to select locations based on verified performance metrics rather than simple guesswork.
